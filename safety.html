Safety

Your safety is the product.

Heidi's vetting process is not an afterthought. It is the foundation the platform is built on. Every decision we make — from who we approve to how sessions are structured — is designed to protect the people who use this platform.

How we vet companions

Before any companion can accept a session on Heidi, they complete every step of this process. There are no shortcuts and no exceptions.

1

Government ID verification

Every companion submits a valid government-issued ID through our secure third-party verification partner. Identity is confirmed before the application moves forward.

2

Criminal history and sex offender registry screening

A comprehensive criminal background check is run, including a mandatory screen against the national sex offender registry. Any match results in immediate and permanent disqualification — no appeals, no exceptions.

3

Video interview with the Heidi team

Every applicant completes a live video interview with a member of the Heidi team. This is not automated. We speak with every companion before they are approved.

4

Signed Companion Agreement and non-sexual services acknowledgment

Companions sign a binding agreement that explicitly acknowledges the non-sexual nature of all services offered on the platform. Violations of this agreement result in immediate removal.

5

Ongoing monitoring

Where legally permitted, we conduct periodic re-screening to ensure companion records remain clear throughout their time on the platform. Safety is not a one-time check.

Client protections

  • First sessions default to public locations during business hours
  • Pre-session boundary agreement required for every booking
  • In-app messaging monitored for policy violations
  • Report button available during all active sessions
  • Rating and review system, moderated by Heidi

Our non-sexual services policy

Heidi is strictly a platform for peer-style emotional support and companionship. No sexual services, explicit content, or romantic arrangements are permitted under any circumstances. Any companion or client who violates this policy is immediately and permanently removed from the platform. There is no path to reinstatement. For the full terms governing conduct on Heidi, see our Code of Conduct.

When something goes wrong

If you experience or witness a safety violation during or after a session, report it immediately using the in-app report button or by contacting us directly. Every report is reviewed by a member of the Heidi team — not an automated system.

We respond to all safety reports within 2 hours during active session windows.

When a report is received, Heidi will: acknowledge receipt, suspend the reported account pending investigation, conduct a review of session records and messaging logs, and communicate findings and next steps to the reporting party within 48 hours.

To report a safety concern, contact us at: info@pleaseheidi.com

Have a concern? We want to hear from you.

You do not need to be a registered user to report a concern. All reports are confidential and reviewed by a person, not a bot.
