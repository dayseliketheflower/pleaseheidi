Safety

Safety isn't a feature. It's the foundation.

Every provider on Heidi has been verified. Every session is protected by clear consent frameworks and a platform that takes violations seriously.

Provider Vetting Process

Before any provider can accept a session, they complete a rigorous multi-step verification process. There are no shortcuts, no exceptions.

1

Government ID Verification

Every provider submits a valid government-issued ID through our secure third-party verification partner. Name, photo, and identity are confirmed before any application moves forward.

2

Criminal History Screening — All 50 States

A comprehensive criminal background check is run across all 50 states. Any felony convictions disqualify an applicant immediately. Heidi covers the cost of this check for all providers.

3

Sex Offender Registry Check — Mandatory, No Exceptions

Every provider is screened against the national sex offender registry before they are approved. A match results in immediate and permanent disqualification — no appeals, no exceptions.

4

Ongoing Monitoring

Where legally permitted, we conduct periodic re-screening to ensure provider records remain clear throughout their time on the platform. Safety is not a one-time box to check.

5

Provider Orientation (~2 hours, self-paced)

Before going live, every provider completes our orientation training covering: consent frameworks, boundary-setting, session best practices, reporting procedures, and platform rules. Completion is required — not optional.

Session Safety Framework

  • First sessions always take place in public locations or professional settings during business hours
  • Private sessions only available after mutual positive session history and explicit consent from both parties
  • Session-specific boundary agreements signed before every meeting — expectations are documented, not assumed
  • Optional pre-session video call available to establish comfort before meeting in person
  • In-app secure messaging monitored for boundary violations and inappropriate requests
  • Panic/report button accessible during every active session for immediate escalation

🧡 For Clients

  • Identity verification required before booking any session
  • Fraud and risk screening to protect providers and the platform
  • Provider ratings and reviews visible to all clients
  • Anonymous reporting available 24/7 — no account required to file a report

🛡️ For Providers

  • Clear platform rules protect you — not just clients
  • You may decline any session for any reason, at any time
  • Support team available if a client behaves inappropriately
  • Documented incident workflow and dispute resolution process available to all providers

What Heidi Is Not

Heidi is not a therapy service, escort service, dating platform, or sexual services marketplace. All services are strictly non-sexual. Any provider or client who violates this is immediately and permanently removed from the platform — with no recourse for reinstatement.
