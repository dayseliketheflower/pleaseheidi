Privacy Policy

We collect only what we need, protect what we hold, and never sell your data. Here is exactly what that means.

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

1. What We Collect

When you use Heidi, we collect information necessary to operate a safe platform. This includes:

  • Name, email address, and phone number provided during registration
  • City or general location (used for matching; we do not track precise location)
  • Government-issued identity documents submitted during KYC verification
  • Session data including date, duration, location type, and boundary agreements
  • In-app messages between clients and providers
  • Reports, ratings, and reviews submitted by users
  • Device and usage data collected automatically (browser type, IP address, page views)

2. How We Use It

We use the information we collect to:

  • Verify identity and screen providers before approval
  • Match clients with available providers in their area
  • Facilitate bookings, session agreements, and communications
  • Monitor in-app messaging for policy violations
  • Investigate reports and enforce platform rules
  • Send transactional communications (booking confirmations, policy updates)
  • Improve platform safety and user experience

We do not use your data to serve you advertising, and we do not build advertising profiles from your activity on Heidi.

3. Who We Share It With

We do not sell your data. We do not share it for advertising purposes. Full stop.

We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With background check and identity verification partners — only with your explicit consent during onboarding, and only the information required to complete the check
  • With law enforcement or regulators, if required by law or court order
  • With payment processors, once payment infrastructure is live — limited to the minimum data required to complete a transaction
  • With service providers who help us operate the platform (hosting, email delivery), under strict data processing agreements

All third-party partners are contractually prohibited from using your data for their own purposes.

4. Data Retention

We keep data only as long as necessary:

  • Session records are retained for 36 months following session completion
  • Safety incidents and related records are retained indefinitely
  • Deleted accounts are cleared from active systems within 30 days of deletion request, subject to retention requirements for safety incidents or legal holds
  • KYC documents are deleted as soon as verification is complete, unless a safety incident requires their retention

5. Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have rights over your personal data, including:

  • The right to access the data we hold about you
  • The right to correct inaccurate information
  • The right to request deletion of your account and associated data
  • The right to data portability (receiving a copy of your data in a usable format)
  • The right to object to certain processing, including profiling

These rights apply to users in the EU and California (under GDPR and CCPA respectively), and we extend them in good faith to all users. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@pleaseheidi.com.

6. Security

We take security seriously, particularly given the sensitive nature of the data we hold:

  • All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest
  • Access to KYC documents is strictly limited to the vetting team and logged
  • Audit logs are maintained for all access to sensitive user records
  • We conduct regular reviews of access controls and security practices

No system is perfectly secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at info@pleaseheidi.com.

7. Cookies

We use a minimal set of cookies to keep the platform functioning — session authentication and basic analytics (page views, errors). We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party behavioral analytics.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Doing so may affect Platform functionality.

8. Children

Heidi is strictly for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If we learn we have done so, we will delete that information immediately. If you believe a minor has registered, contact us at info@pleaseheidi.com.

9. Contact

Privacy questions, data requests, and concerns can be sent to info@pleaseheidi.com. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
