1. What We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you sign up, verify your identity, or use the platform — as well as certain information generated automatically when you use our services.

CategoryExamples
Account informationName, email address, phone number, city
Identity verificationGovernment-issued ID (processed by our third-party partner)
Provider application dataAge, gender identity, sexual orientation (optional), motivation statement, services offered
Session dataBooking history, session timing, location type (public/private), ratings and reviews
CommunicationsIn-app messages, support emails, report submissions
Usage dataPages visited, device type, browser, IP address, referral source

We do not collect financial account details directly — payment processing is handled by our secure third-party payment provider.

2. How We Use It

We use the information we collect to:

  • Verify your identity and run background screening (providers)
  • Facilitate bookings and session management between clients and providers
  • Send confirmation emails, platform updates, and safety notifications
  • Monitor the platform for violations of our Terms of Use and Code of Conduct
  • Improve our product and resolve technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to law enforcement requests where required

We do not use your personal information for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties. We do not build advertising profiles based on your usage of Heidi.

3. Who We Share It With

We share your data only in limited circumstances:

  • Identity & background check partners: To complete required verification for providers. These partners handle data under strict confidentiality agreements and applicable law.
  • Payment processors: To complete transactions securely. We do not store full payment card information.
  • Infrastructure providers: Cloud hosting and data storage (e.g., AWS). These providers do not have permission to use your data for any purpose other than providing services to Heidi.
  • Law enforcement: When legally required by valid subpoena, court order, or to prevent imminent harm.

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers. We do not use third-party advertising on Heidi.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you close your account, we delete or anonymize personal data within 90 days, except where we are legally required to retain it (for example, records related to background check compliance, payment transactions, or safety incidents).

Identity documents submitted for verification are handled by our third-party partner and are typically deleted from their systems within 30 days of the verification decision. We do not retain copies of government-issued IDs on Heidi servers.

Session data (booking records, ratings) may be retained for up to 3 years for platform safety and dispute resolution purposes.

5. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Correction

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

Deletion

Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.

Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Objection

Object to certain processing of your data.

No Sale

California residents: we do not sell your personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@pleaseheidi.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Measures we use include:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS) for all data exchanged with our platform
  • Encryption at rest for sensitive data fields
  • Access controls — only team members who need your data to perform their role can access it
  • Regular security reviews and vulnerability assessments

No system is perfectly secure. If you have reason to believe your data has been compromised, please contact us immediately at privacy@pleaseheidi.com.

7. Cookies

Heidi uses a minimal set of cookies necessary for the platform to function — for example, to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics that profile you across the web.

You can configure your browser to refuse cookies, though doing so may affect your ability to use certain features of the platform.

8. Children

Heidi is strictly for adults aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we discover that a user is under 18, we will immediately terminate their account and delete any associated data. If you believe a minor has registered, please contact us at safety@pleaseheidi.com.

9. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, data requests, or concerns, contact us at:

privacy@pleaseheidi.com

We aim to respond to all privacy requests within 30 days.
