How It Works

Simple, safe, and on your terms.

Getting support shouldn't be complicated. Heidi makes it straightforward to find the right person at the right time.

1

Sign up & verify

Create your profile. We verify your identity so every person on the platform is who they say they are — on both sides.

2

Browse vetted providers

Read profiles, reviews, and service offerings. Every provider has passed a background check, ID verification, and our orientation training.

3

Book a session on your terms

Choose the type of support, location, and time that works for you. Set expectations together before the session begins.

What Heidi Offers

Real presence for real life.

Heidi is for the moments your therapist can't be there — and for needs that don't fit neatly into a clinical appointment.

🏥

Medical & Appointment Accompaniment

Someone to sit with you at a difficult appointment, drive you home, or just be present when you can't face it alone.

💙

Emotional Presence During Hard Times

A compassionate companion when you're grieving, overwhelmed, or just need someone to witness what you're going through.

☕

Public Companionship

Walks, errands, outings — a warm presence in everyday life for those moments when isolation creeps in.

🤲

Platonic Physical Comfort

Hand-holding, sitting close, being held — only ever if desired, always within clear, agreed boundaries.

🎧

Listening Sessions

No advice unless asked. No fixing. Just a real person giving you their full, undivided attention.

Safety First

Safety isn't a feature. It's the foundation.

Every person on Heidi has been verified. Every session is protected by clear consent frameworks — and a platform that takes violations seriously.

🔍

Thorough Vetting

Every provider completes government ID verification, criminal history screening, and sex offender registry checks across all 50 states.

📋

Consent Frameworks

Session-specific boundary agreements before every meeting. Clear rules. Clear consequences. No exceptions.

🚨

Real-Time Protection

A report button during every active session, 24/7 support access, and zero tolerance for any violation of our non-sexual policy.

For Providers

Apply to become a Heidi provider

Providers are thoroughly vetted and trained. This is a meaningful way to earn income while offering real human support — with clear boundaries and a strong safety framework backing you up.

📋

Application received — thanks, .

Here's exactly what to expect next:

✅

STEP 1 — Application Received (today)

You'll receive a confirmation email at with a summary of your application and resources.

🔍

STEP 2 — Identity Verification (within 48 hours)

A member of our team will reach out to initiate government ID verification via our secure third-party partner. Takes ~10 min on your end.

📋

STEP 3 — Background Check (3–5 business days)

Comprehensive criminal history check including sex offender registry screening. Required for all providers — no exceptions. We cover the cost.

🎓

STEP 4 — Orientation & Training (self-paced, ~2 hours)

Once cleared, you'll complete our online provider orientation covering boundary-setting, consent frameworks, safety protocols, and session best practices.

🚀

STEP 5 — Profile Setup & Launch

Set your rates, availability, and services — then go live in your market.
