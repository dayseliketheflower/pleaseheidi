FAQ

Common questions.

Is this therapy?+

No. Heidi companions are not licensed therapists and do not provide clinical mental health services. If you are in crisis, please contact a mental health professional or call 988.

Is this sexual in any way?+

No. All sessions are governed by a written Non-Sexual Services Agreement. Any companion who violates this policy is immediately and permanently removed from the platform.

Who are Heidi companions?+

Companions are vetted individuals — primarily men, and some women — who have passed identity verification and background screening. They are trained in active listening, boundary-setting, and professional conduct.

Where do sessions take place?+

First sessions default to public locations during normal business hours. Private locations are available after both parties have established a positive history on the platform.

How much does it cost?+

Pricing will be announced at launch. Heidi is currently accepting waitlist signups.
