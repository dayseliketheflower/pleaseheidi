How it works

Five steps from signup to session.

01

Sign up & verify

Create an account and verify your identity through Persona — takes about 2 minutes. Every Heidi client is verified before they can browse companions.

02

Start with a $20 Meet

Every new client-companion pairing begins with a mandatory Meet session — 30 minutes, public location, $20. Meet your companion in person before committing to anything longer. Either party can decline to continue, no penalty.

03

Book a session

After your Meet, all session tiers unlock with that companion. Choose your length (60, 90, or 120 min), set your touch preferences (hugs, handshakes, or no physical contact — you decide), and complete the boundary agreement before confirming.

04

Session

Your companion arrives. The 'I need help' button is visible on every screen throughout your session. Automated check-in at 45 minutes for sessions over an hour — if you don't respond within 10 minutes, our team is alerted.

05

Review

Leave a rating after every session. Anonymous reviews help other women find the right companion. A safety check is included — if anything felt off, you can file an incident report directly from the review flow.

Safety

Every layer of protection — built in from day one.

Heidi's safety infrastructure isn't a policy document. It's structural. Every feature of the platform — from the mandatory first Meet to the in-session panic button — was built with your physical and emotional safety as the first constraint.

Mandatory $20 Meet session

Every new client-companion pairing starts with a public, 30-minute Meet before any private or longer session is possible. This is a structural safety feature — not optional, not waivable.

ID verification

Every companion verifies their identity through Persona before their profile goes live. Every client is verified before they can book.

Criminal background check

Full national criminal history plus sex offender registry search on every companion. Any sex offense is a permanent rejection.

"I need help" button

Visible on every screen during an active session. One tap opens three options: immediate operator alert (within 30 seconds), session end, or incident report.

Automated check-ins

At 45 minutes into any session over 60 minutes, the app checks in. No response within 10 minutes triggers an automatic operator alert with your location and emergency contact.

Boundary agreements

Touch preferences, topics to avoid, and the nonsexual services policy are confirmed by both parties before every booking — not just at signup.
