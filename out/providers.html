What companions do

Show up. Be present. Get paid.

Appointment accompaniment

Accompany clients to medical appointments, procedures, and clinical visits. Sit with them in the waiting room. Be the calm presence in the room when the news is hard.

Emotional presence

Show up during hard weeks — grief, burnout, anxiety, life transitions. You listen, you stay present, you don't try to fix anything.

Steady Companion

For nursing students only. Bring your clinical familiarity to medical visit accompaniment. Not clinical care — informed human presence at a premium rate.

Practical support

Errand help, task assistance, or a grounded presence during a chaotic day. Practical and personal.

The process

What happens after you apply.

Every companion goes through six steps before their listing goes live. This is what makes clients feel safe enough to show up.

1
Application review
Your personal statement and information are reviewed by the Heidi team within 1–2 business days.
2
Identity verification
Government ID + selfie liveness check via Persona. Takes under 10 minutes on your end.
3
Background check
Full criminal history and sex offender registry screening via Checkr. Required for every companion, no exceptions. Cost: $35–60, paid by companion.
4
Social media review
Manual review by the Heidi team. Documented and kept confidential.
5
Video interview
A 20–30 minute conversation with the Heidi team. We want to know who you are.
6
Companion training
Self-paced orientation covering consent, boundary-setting, session safety, and how to handle distress. Acknowledged in writing before your listing goes live.
