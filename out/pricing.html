FAQ

Pricing questions

Is the $20 Meet session refundable?+

The $20 Meet session is non-refundable. The full $20 goes to your companion. It is not a deposit and does not apply toward future sessions.

Are discounts applied automatically?+

Yes. The 15% repeat discount and 10% birthday discount are applied at checkout automatically — no code needed. The 5-session bundle is purchased upfront at 20% off.

Does the 5-session bundle apply to all tiers?+

Yes. The 5-session bundle applies to all session tiers, including Nursing Companion sessions.

Are there cancellation fees?+

Cancellation policies will be published at launch. They are being designed to be fair to both clients and companions.

Is client access free?+

Yes. There is no membership, subscription, or fee to join, browse, or book. You pay per session only.
